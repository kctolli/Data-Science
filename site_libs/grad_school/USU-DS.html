Data Science - MS

Print-Friendly Page
   

Return to: College of Science

College of Science

Department of Computer Science

Requirements for the Masters of Science in Data Science are listed below. Students must complete 30 credit hours. All students will complete a Plan A MS which includes the completion of a Thesis that will be reviewed by their committee. 

Required Courses

Students must complete all of these courses.

  • CS 5665 - Introduction to Data Science 3
  • CS 5800 - Introduction to Database Systems 3

  •  

  • CS 5830 - Data Science Incubator 3 or
  • CS 6830 - Data Science Incubator 3

  •  

  • CS 6665 - Data Mining 3
  • CS 6675 - Advanced Data Science and Mining 3
  • CS 6800 - Advanced Database Systems 3
  • CS 6970 - Thesis and Research 1-9

Elective Courses

Students select two of these courses.

  • CS 6250 - Cooperative Work Experience, Graduate 1-9
  • CS 6600 - AI: Advanced Intelligent Systems 4
  • CS 6680 - Computer Vision: Foundations and Applications 3
  • CS 6890 - Topics in Computer Science (Topic) 1-4
  • STAT 5810 - Topics in Statistics 1-3
  • STAT 6550 - Statistical Computing 3
  • STAT 6650 - Stat Learning: Multivariate Stat Analysis for Bioinformatics, Data Mining, and Machine Learning 3
